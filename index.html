Sean B. Lynch

About

I am currently a postdoc in Ivan Fesenko's Higher Number Theory Group at Westlake University in Hangzhou, China. My research at Westlake is about two-dimensional zeta integrals.

Daniel Chan supervised my previous postdoc appointment at the University of New South Wales in Sydney, Australia. He and Liangyi Zhao are my mathematical fathers.
