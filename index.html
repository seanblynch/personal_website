About

I am currently a postdoc at UNSW Sydney, working with Daniel Chan. I will soon be a postdoc at Westlake University, working with Ivan Fesenko.

My research is in number theory, e.g. exponential sums and zeta functions. Daniel Chan and Liangyi Zhao are my mathematical fathers.
