    I am a postdoc in Ivan Fesenko's Higher number theory group at the Institute for Theoretical Sciences, Westlake University in Hangzhou, China. Daniel Chan supervised my previous postdoc appointment at the University of New South Wales in Sydney, Australia. He and Liangyi Zhao are my mathematical fathers. 

    My research at ITS is about two-dimensional zeta integrals.
